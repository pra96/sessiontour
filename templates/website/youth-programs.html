Youth Climbing Programs

We have a variety of youth climbing programs for children of all ages and skill levels. Check out the programs we have to offer and reach out to our coaches for more information!

Session Masters

This is an invitation only team that represents Sessions Climbing + Fitness. Session Masters are sponsored by the gym and are hand selected by your trained coaching staff from any of the youth team categories.

Session Elites

The Session Elites is for athletes driven by competition, highly motivated, and who love climbing. Athletes qualify before joining the Session Elite Team. Session Elites are split into two training sections: Elite A for ages 12-19 and Elite B for ages 5-12.

Session Experts - Group A

This is an open team that accepts all athletes. The purpose of the Session Experts is to prepare athletes for indoor climbing competitions in a variety of disciplines. Age restriction for youth team Recreational A is 16-19.

Session Experts - Group B

This is an open team that accepts all athletes. The purpose of the Session Experts is to prepare athletes for indoor climbing competitions in a variety of disciplines. Age restriction for youth team Recreational A is 12-15.

Session Scouts - Group A

The Session Scouts will focus on learning the fundamentals of climbing and how to improve their climbing IQ, movement, strength, and mental fortitude. Age restriction for Session Scouts - Group A is 9-11.

Session Scouts - Group B

The Session Scouts will focus on learning the fundamentals of climbing and how to improve their climbing IQ, movement, strength, and mental fortitude. Age restriction for Session Scouts - Group A is 5-9.
